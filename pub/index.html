About Joe


Originally born and raised in Canada, Joe relocated to the US in 2000 to access the explosive opportunities in tech. After working for a brief time in the New York Adtech world, Joe moved to California to join the computer security industry in 2001. In 2014, Joe took time off to pursue passion projects in independent filmmaking. In 2015, Joe pivoted to join the burgeoning community growing around Virtual Reality. Most recently Joe has begun investing in and mentoring seed stage startups.
